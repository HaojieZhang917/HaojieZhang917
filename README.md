## 🔬 About Me

I am a Master's student specializing in **Fluid Mechanics** at **Tianjin University (TJU)**. My passion lies at the intersection of fluid dynamics, applied mathematics, and high-performance scientific computing.

- 🎯 **Research Interests**: Hydrodynamic Stability Theory (LST, BiGlobal/TriGlobal), Compressible Boundary Layer Transition, Receptivity Mechanisms, and Asymptotic Analysis.
- 💻 **Numerical Expertise**: High-precision numerical methods (Chebyshev spectral methods, Gauss-Lobatto-Legendre points, compact finite difference schemes).
- 🚀 **HPC Focus**: Developing scalable solvers for large-scale banded matrices and matrix eigenvalue problems using distributed-memory parallel architecture.

## 📁 Featured Research Repositories

### 🛠️ [TwoDiskStabilityCore.jl](https://github.com/HaojieZhang917) 
*A Julia-based high-precision toolkit developed for the local linear stability and adjoint-based receptivity analysis of rotating disk boundary layers.*
- Built-in matrix differential operators via **Chebyshev spectral methods** and mapped coordinate grids.
- Integrated with state-of-the-art sparse solvers and Krylov subspace iterative techniques.
- Configured with automated **GitHub Actions CI/CD workflows** for rigorous cross-platform testing.

### 🌊 [FlowLocalStabilityCore](https://github.com/HaojieZhang917) `Software Copyright`
*A general-purpose toolkit registered for local flow stability research, optimizing high-order discretization schemes for compressible boundary layers.*
